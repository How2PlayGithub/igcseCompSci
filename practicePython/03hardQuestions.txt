Create a simple Banking System

In the 1D array, it includes the account holders name that are in the system. In the 2D array, it
includes the card number, account number, and the amount of money in the account. The position of
each account's data in the two arrays are the same. 

Get the input of the accoutn holders name, output the:
card number
account number
amount of money
And then ask if they would want to deposit or withdraw money.
If they want to deposit money, add the money to the account and output the new amount of money.
If they want to withdraw money, subtract the money from the account and output the new amount of money.
